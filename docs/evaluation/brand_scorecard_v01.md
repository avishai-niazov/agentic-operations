# Brand Scorecard v01

## Scored dimensions

- unmistakably Planerium
- calm, clear, credible tone
- non-generic structure
- enrichment-first value
- trust and originality
- low filler risk
- reusable exemplar potential

## Disqualifiers

- interchangeable with mass AI content
- inflated tone
- vague educational promise
- SEO-first wording with weak usefulness
