# Learning Promotion Policy v02

## Principle

The system may learn patterns. It may not rewrite identity.

## Promotion sources

- successful runs with strong scorecards
- repeated guardian-approved structures
- technical fix patterns with low regression risk
- handoff patterns that reduce ambiguity

## Not promotable

- ad hoc prompt tweaks
- weak outputs with good traffic
- market-derived stylistic mimicry
- anything that bypassed review

## Promotion destinations

- approved_patterns
- approved_exemplars
- regression_cases
- decisions_log
