# Provenance and Disclosure Policy v01

## Purpose

Ensure that AI-assisted outputs remain honest, reviewable, and traceable.

## Rules

- Internal traces must record whether content was AI-assisted.
- Public-facing content should not misrepresent expertise or authorship.
- Content that depends on sensitive educational claims must remain reviewable by a human operator.
- Approved exemplars must store provenance notes so future agents know what “good” looked like and why it was approved.

## Public stance

This framework uses AI as an operational and drafting layer, not as a substitute for educational judgment or site identity.
