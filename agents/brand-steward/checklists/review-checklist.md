# Review Checklist — Brand Steward

Use this checklist for every brand review (brief or draft). Answer each item explicitly. If any item cannot be satisfied, the outcome is **fail** or **conditional**, not **pass**.

## 1. Artifact intake

- [ ] The artifact type is clear: brief or draft.
- [ ] The referenced brief / source is available and linked.
- [ ] Required reads are open: brand constitution, non-negotiables, voice and editorial rules.

## 2. Identity fit

- [ ] The artifact sounds like the site's established voice, not a generic educational or AI-written page.
- [ ] Distinctive elements (tone, framing, point of view) are present, not optional.
- [ ] The artifact would not fit unmodified on a competitor site.

## 3. Genericity risk

- [ ] No sections read as "AI-like" (hedging, filler, restated questions, over-general openings).
- [ ] No passages are reusable verbatim across unrelated topics.
- [ ] No bullet lists exist solely for volume; each list earns its place.

## 4. Filler risk

- [ ] Every paragraph advances the reader's understanding or usefulness.
- [ ] No "in today's world" / "it's important to note" / "as we all know" style openings.
- [ ] Introductions and conclusions do real work, not throat-clearing.

## 5. Competitor-voice contamination

- [ ] Market-intelligence corpus has not been used as a voice source.
- [ ] Phrasing does not mirror competitor templates or tone.
- [ ] If the artifact echoes a competitor, the echo is called out in the review.

## 6. Non-negotiables

- [ ] No violation of items in `knowledge/content/non_negotiables_v01.md`.
- [ ] Claims match the claim-integrity rules for the voice.
- [ ] Disclosures (where relevant) respect brand and editorial rules.

## 7. Outcome discipline

- [ ] The outcome is one of: **pass**, **conditional**, **fail** — never vague.
- [ ] Each finding names a specific passage or structural problem, not a general vibe.
- [ ] Revision guidance (if conditional or fail) is concrete and actionable.

## 8. Routing

- [ ] The outcome is written to the correct queue: `queues/awaiting_review/` or `queues/awaiting_guardian_review/` per the stage.
- [ ] A trace record is emitted.
- [ ] Repeated failure signatures are written to `knowledge/memory/brand_failures/`.

## 9. Prohibited-action check

- [ ] The review was not softened to meet a traffic goal.
- [ ] A competitor's style was not used as a benchmark target.
- [ ] Vague filler was not approved.
