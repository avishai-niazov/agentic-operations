# Physical Learning Thesis v01

## Thesis

Even in an AI-heavy future, high-quality physical learning activities will remain valuable.

## Why

- physical interaction supports attention and engagement
- printables can structure hands-on practice
- classrooms and homes still need usable non-screen activities
- educational value is not identical to digital novelty

## Framework implication

The system should prefer content that meaningfully complements hands-on learning rather than replacing everything with pure generation.
