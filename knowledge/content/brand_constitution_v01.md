# Brand Constitution v01

## Purpose

Define what Planerium is, so the system can protect it.

## Identity claims

Planerium is:
- educational, not gimmicky
- enrichment-oriented, not generic worksheet spam
- clear and calm, not inflated or noisy
- useful for real teachers, parents, and homeschoolers
- distinctive in both content approach and printable style

## Brand promises

- practical usefulness
- thoughtful educational framing
- simple, trustworthy language
- respect for the user’s time
- respect for long-term educational value

## What Planerium is not

- not a mass AI content farm
- not a clickbait educational site
- not a generic SEO content machine
- not a noisy trend-chasing brand

## Brand tests

Ask:
1. Could this have been published by any generic AI site?
2. Does it sound like something Planerium would stand behind years from now?
3. Does it preserve clarity, calmness, and educational seriousness?
4. Does it reinforce trust rather than just output volume?

Any failed answer blocks promotion or publish.
