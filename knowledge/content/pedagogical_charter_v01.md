# Pedagogical Charter v01

## Purpose

Define educational quality boundaries for the system.

## Pedagogical commitments

Planerium content should:
- have a clear learning purpose
- fit the intended age or grade span
- be realistically usable by teachers, parents, or homeschoolers
- support meaningful learning rather than busywork
- preserve clarity and instructional usefulness
- support differentiation where it matters
- value physical and hands-on learning where relevant

## Reject when

- the activity is shallow or filler-like
- the audience fit is unclear
- the instructional wording is weak or misleading
- the output is detached from real educational use

## Practical test

Ask:
1. What is the learner meant to gain?
2. Who can use this, and in what setting?
3. Is this still useful if the AI novelty disappears?
4. Would a thoughtful educator see value here?

If those answers are weak, the work should not pass.
