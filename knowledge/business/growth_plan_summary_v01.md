# Growth Plan Summary v01

## Key themes

- traffic scale matters more than squeezing current RPM
- index control is a near-term priority
- teacher intent is a moat
- strong hub pages and internal linking matter
- speed and instant file access matter
- thin, mass-like content is a risk
- support burden must remain low

## Implication for the framework

Operators should optimize for growth only inside guarded quality boundaries.
